# DiamondOps Liability Boundary

## Scope of Responsibility

DiamondOps provides technical determinations, validation frameworks, and operational documentation produced under its published schemas at the time of issuance.

DiamondOps is responsible solely for the correctness and internal consistency of those technical determinations and documentation, given the information available at issuance.

## Explicit Exclusions

DiamondOps does not:

- manufacture, sell, supply, or distribute physical equipment
- install, commission, operate, or maintain equipment
- control, actuate, or override equipment or safety systems
- guarantee real-world performance, output, yield, or safety outcomes
- replace regulatory authority, inspection, or enforcement

All physical installation, operation, and equipment performance remain the responsibility of the asset owner, equipment manufacturer, and/or designated contractors.

## Reliance and Use

DiamondOps documentation is intended to support decision-making, compliance preparation, and auditability. Final implementation decisions and operational use remain the responsibility of the deploying parties.

## Versioning

This liability boundary applies to all DiamondOps outputs produced under schemas and versions in effect at the time of issuance.
